Chandrayaan : India's Pride

The Chandrayaan programme also known as the Indian Lunar Exploration Program is an ongoing series of outer space missions by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) for the exploration of the Moon.

The Chandrayaan

Chandrayaan 1 which means moon-craft was the first lunar probe under the programme chandrayaan. It was launched by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) in October 2008

Chandrayaan 2 which is the second lunar exploration mission developed by the ISRO, after Chandrayaan-1. It consists of a lunar orbiter.

Chandrayaan 3 which is the second lunar exploration mission developed by the ISRO, after Chandrayaan-1. It consists of a lunar orbiter.
